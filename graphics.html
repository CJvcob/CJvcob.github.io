Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaulated: Apple

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on the Apple home page are simple, yet complex at the same time. Also it helps Apple get their products to the customer and make it visually appleaing so the customer can buy it. The most prominent image is the big images in the center of the page. It includes a clean font that says their product name and a clear, hd image of their products. At the bottom it advertises off their other products to buy. They use simple colors like black and white to keep it simple yet clean.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics fit the purpose, organization, and the style of the important content on the page.
  2. Yes - Graphics help to guide the viewers' focus onto the important content of the page.
  3. Yes - Avoid overlight bright and potentially "obnoxious" images.
  4. Yes - Alternate text is present for all images.
